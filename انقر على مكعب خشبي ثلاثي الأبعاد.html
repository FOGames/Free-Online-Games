Tap 3D Wood Block Away is a challenging and addictive puzzle game where you rotate and tap to clear blocks, unlocking complex 3D structures. With intuitive controls, simply swipe to rotate and tap to remove cubes in the correct order. Progress through increasingly tricky levels, sharpen your spatial thinking, and enjoy a satisfying puzzle experience at your own pace!
