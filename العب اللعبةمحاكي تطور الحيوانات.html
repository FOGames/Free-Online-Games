Evolve and go from a worm to the highest stage of evolution. Eat plants and mushrooms, grow and fight predators. Choose your strategy - should you be a stealthy hunter or a mighty defender of your territory? Experience the delights of evolution, upgrade your skills and adapt to a changing world. Step over the threshold of the next stage of evolution and find out what awaits you next in the next biome! - Eat, fight predators and survive in a dangerous world! - Evolve and rise to the highest level of development! - Explore the map! - Discover new biomes! Good luck in Evolution!
طريقه اللعب
Control the creature, eat mushrooms and plants, attack predators and evolve. PC Management: WASD - moving Space - jump The left mouse button is an attack Right mouse button - moving the camera Mouse wheel - zoom in/out of the camera Smartphone controls: Left joystick - moving Button with a jumping man - jump The target button is an attack Swipe on the left side of the screen to move the camera
